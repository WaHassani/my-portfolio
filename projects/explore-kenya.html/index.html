Top Destinations in Kenya

Maasai Mara

Experience the Great Migration and the vast savannah of Kenya’s famous Maasai Mara National Reserve.

Diani Beach

Relax at the pristine beaches of Diani, one of Africa’s top beach destinations.

Mount Kenya

Climb Africa’s second-highest peak and marvel at the breathtaking views of Mount Kenya.

Kenya's Events & Festivals

Lamu Cultural Festival

Join this annual festival celebrating the rich culture of Lamu, a UNESCO World Heritage site, with traditional music, dances, and dhow races.

The Great Migration

Witness the awe-inspiring migration of over 1.5 million wildebeest and zebras between the Maasai Mara and the Serengeti, one of the greatest natural spectacles on earth.

Safari Rally

Feel the thrill of the iconic Safari Rally, a high-speed adventure across Kenya’s rugged terrains that attracts motorsport enthusiasts worldwide.
